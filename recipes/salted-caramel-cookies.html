Salted Caramel Cookies Recipe

A delicious sea salt caramel chocolate chip cookie recipe from Hersheys using salted caramel chips on a chocolate cookie dough.
Source: Link

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cups cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 2/3 cups salted caramel baking chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 °F.
  2. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  3. Make the cookie dough:
    • Beat butter, brown sugar, sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until well mixed.
    • Add eggs and mix until blended.
    • On a separate bowl, mix flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
    • Gradually blend dry mixture into the butter mixture, blend well.
    • Stir in salted caramel chips.
  4. Drop rounded teaspoons of dough onto cookie sheets.
  5. Bake for 7-9 min. Do not over bake.
  6. Cool slightly and then cool cookies completely on a wire rack.
