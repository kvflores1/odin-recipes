Chewy Brownies Recipe

The best chewy brownies with a rich chocolate flavor recipe by Tessa Arias.
Source: Link

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, cold
  • 1 egg yolk, cold
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 325°F.
  2. Line an 8 by 8 inch pan with parchment paper and spray with oil.
  3. Microwave butter and sugar for 1 min, or until butter is melted. Whisk in eggs, egg yolk, and vanilla extract. Stir oil and cocoa powder into the mixture.
  4. With spatula stir in flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt until combined. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Spread batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 30 min, or until the brownies are set, and a skewer inserted into the center or the brownie has moist crumbs attached. Do not over-bake.
  7. Let cool completely to solidify before cutting and serving.
